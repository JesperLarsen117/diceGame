RULES


Each player roll the dice and move their character along the green spaces, according to the number of eyes shown on the dice.
If a player lands on a purple space they're given a question and 3 possible answers. If the player picks the right answer, they get to roll the dice again. If not, the turn switches to the next player.
If a player manages to land on the blue space, they are allowed to skip all the way down to the second blue space.
The winner is the player who first reaches the ending space.

Have fun playing!
